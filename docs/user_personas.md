## User Personas

PCOSense is designed as an analytics-driven decision-support solution for multiple stakeholders involved in women’s health, wellness, and population-level care.

---

### Persona 1: Preventive Healthcare Analyst

**Role:** Data Analyst / Public Health Analyst  
**Organization Type:** Healthcare providers, NGOs, research institutions  

**Goals:**
- Identify early risk patterns in PCOS-related indicators
- Track population-level trends over time
- Support preventive health initiatives using data

**Challenges:**
- Fragmented datasets
- Lack of unified metrics
- Difficulty translating data into actionable insights

**How PCOSense Helps:**
- Aggregated risk scoring
- Trend-based dashboards
- Visual correlation analysis using Tableau Cloud

---

### Persona 2: Wellness Program Manager

**Role:** Corporate Wellness Lead / Women’s Health Program Manager  
**Organization Type:** Enterprises, universities, wellness platforms  

**Goals:**
- Monitor anonymized health indicators
- Design targeted wellness interventions
- Track improvement metrics without medical diagnosis

**Challenges:**
- No early warning system
- Over-reliance on self-reported feedback
- Low engagement with static reports

**How PCOSense Helps:**
- Visual risk indicators
- Alerts when risk thresholds rise
- Actionable dashboards for intervention planning

---

### Persona 3: Research & Policy Advisor

**Role:** Health Researcher / Policy Analyst  
**Organization Type:** Government bodies, academic institutions  

**Goals:**
- Understand PCOS prevalence patterns
- Study correlations between lifestyle and symptoms
- Support evidence-based policymaking

**Challenges:**
- Data interpretation complexity
- Communication gaps between data and policy

**How PCOSense Helps:**
- Clear, explainable metrics
- Population-level analytics
- Tableau-powered storytelling dashboards

---

### Persona 4: Individual Insight Consumer (Non-Clinical)

**Role:** Health-conscious individual  
**Usage Context:** Educational and self-awareness purposes only  

**Goals:**
- Understand patterns in health indicators
- Improve lifestyle awareness
- Engage with visual, easy-to-understand analytics

**Important Note:**
This persona does not receive diagnoses or medical recommendations. Insights are informational and educational only.
